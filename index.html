IKAROS

THE LEGEND OF IKAROS

Driven by the desire for freedom, Ikaros took to the sky on wings of wax and feathers. The path was narrow: fly too low, and the sea would dampen the wings; fly too high, and the sun’s heat would destroy them. In the thrill of the ascent, Ikaros reached for the sun. The wings dissolved, and the flight ended in the abyss.

HOW TO PLAY

  • ORBIT: Your momentum is captured by the gravity of the stars.
  • LEAP: Tap the screen to break the orbit and dash toward the sun.
  • CLIMB: You can only progress Up. Releasing downward will result in a fall.
  • MELT: The higher you go, the faster the stars shrink. Do not linger.
  • MOMENTUM: Jump quickly between stars to increase your score multiplier.
